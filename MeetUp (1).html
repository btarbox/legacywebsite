Boston AWS MeetUp


I've been a member of the Boston AWS MeetUp for about nine years and the organizer for three years.

We are one of the largest AWS user groups in the world and have been meeting every other week since the Pandemic forced us to switch to virtual meetings. We tend to attract between 40-80 actual attendees per meeting (but have occasionally hit the 100 person Zoom limit).

In the past year we've had meetings on .Net For Cloud, CDK Deep Dive, Jeff Bar, Serverless With Lambda, re:Invent/DLDR, Serverless Java, Chaos Engineering, Terraform v Cloudformation, GraphQL, DocumentDB, DeviceFarm, DynamoDB Secondary Indexes, Intro to ML, EventBridge Deep Dive, and many others.
