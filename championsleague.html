Champions League is an Alexa skill that lets you get information about UEFA Champions League football (soccer for Americans). It is available in the US and the UK. Just ask your Alexa device to "enable Champions League". Then ask it to "open Champions League" and follow the voice prompts.

Champions League in the Amazon Skills Store - enable it or review it!
