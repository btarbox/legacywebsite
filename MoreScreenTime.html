More Screen Time is an Alexa skill to help parents control their childrens' screen time

The child or their parent asks an Alexa device (Amazon Echo, Tap, Dot or FireStick) for more screen time. The skill then asks the child if they have performed each of the chores that has been configured for the morning, evening or weekend. If they say 'yes' to each chore they are granted more screen time. If they say 'no', 'do I have to?', 'maybe' or any other similar kind of answer they are told they more not have more screen time. They can also say "don't have to" if the chore does not apply to them. In this case we will not ask about that chore again.
